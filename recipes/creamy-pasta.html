Creamy Chicken Pasta

Description

A cream sauce chicken pasta with root vegetables and aromatics.
Very creamy and has chicken. There are also vegetables like onion.
Pretty easy to make.

Ingredints

  • 1 large chicken breast
  • 2 cups of spinach
  • half of a large onion
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 stalk of celary
  • 200mL of cream
  • 100g of cheese
  • 350g of penne or spiral pasta
  • Garlic, oregano, paprika, rosemary, salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Dice chicken into large cubes and season with herbs, spices, and aromatics
  2. Cook pasta in a low smoke point oil over medium heat
  3. Place a large pot of water on a high heat to boil, and once boiling add pasta
  4. Dicce oinon, celary, and onion into cubes and, once partially cooked through, add to the chicken
  5. When the chicken and vegetables have cooked through add the cream into the pan and lower the heat
  6. Add the spinach to the crream sauce a handful at a time until cohesive
  7. Drain the pasta and add the sauce into the pot then mix and add the cheese, and salt & pepper to taste
