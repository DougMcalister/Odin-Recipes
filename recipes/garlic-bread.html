Garlic Bread

A recipe for large slice garlic bread. Once made they can be
enjoyed with soups or any pasta dish.The butter is applicable to
any dish and can be stored in the fridge or freezer

Ingredients

  • 150g unsalted butter
  • 2-4 large pieces of turkish bread
  • 2-5 cloves of garlic
  • Oregano, Thyme, Salt, Pepper, and onion powder

Steps

  1. Begin by Wrapping the garlic in alfoil and placing in
    an oven or airffryer at 200°C for 8-10 minutes
  2. While the garlic is roasting place the butter in a heat
    safe bowl and place over hot water and gently
    stir with a spoon until the butter is soft and maliable
  3. Once both the butter has softened but not melted and the
    garlic has roasted, place the cloves in the bowl of butter
    and whiop until homogenised.
  4. While whipping add the herbs and spices to the butter, and
    continue mixing until combined.
  5. Spread the butter on the slices of turkish bread and roat for
    10 minutes at 150°C or until golden brown.
