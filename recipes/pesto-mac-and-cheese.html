Pesto Mac and Cheese

Ingredients

  • 110g or a handful of basil
  • Half a cup of pine nuts
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • A quarter cup of finely grated parmesan
  • 100mL of extra virgin olive oil
  • 200g of pasta (Dealers choice)
  • 50g of sharp cheddar
  • 50g of monterey jack
  • 50g of mozzarella
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Begin by adding basil, pine nuts, olive oil, garlic (chopped),
    and parmesan to a blender and pulse until smooth
  2. Boil pasta until soft and drain.
  3. Pour over the pesto, and add the chees blend then stirthrough until
    gloopy.
  4. Add salt & pepper to taste and serve.
