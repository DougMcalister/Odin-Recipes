Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 200 g of lean minced beef
  • Half arge onion, diced
  • 200g of chunky tomato puree
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Half of a large carrot, diced
  • 1 stalk of celery, diced
  • Half a cup of dry red wine
  • 100g of spaghetti
  • Oregano, Thyme, Rosemary, Salt, and Pepper

Steps

  1. Begin by adding beef mince and herbs to a lightly oiled
    pan and sauteing until light brown.
  2. Once beef and herbs are browned and homogenous add garlic,
    carrot, and cellary too the pan and deglaze with red wine.
  3. Once the beef and vegetables are cooked through lower the heat and
    and add the tomato puree and onions to the pan and stir through,
    then allow to simmer.
  4. Boil water in a medium pot and, once boiling, add the spaghetti and
    boil until soft.
  5. Once the pasta has boiled, drain it and add to the sauce mixture and
    stir through until combined.
